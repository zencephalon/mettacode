Mettā

Mettā is a Pali word meaning “loving-kindness”, an approach towards our lives and our clients that we find great joy and refuge in.

The Story

One day, some hackers who really enjoyed each other’s company thought, “Shouldn’t there be a way for us to all work together and have a great time too?” Thinking about it more, they realized they shared a dream. To work in harmony with the flexibility allowed by trust and personal ownership they could accomplish the improbable: write truly great code.
